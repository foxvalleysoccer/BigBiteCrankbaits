Privacy Policy - Panfish Panic

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Panfish Panic ("the app") is an Android game. This policy explains what data the app handles.

Data the app collects directly

None. The app itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. There are no accounts, no analytics, and no advertising SDKs.

Data handled through Google Play Games Services

The app uses Google Play Games Services to provide sign-in and a global leaderboard. When you choose to sign in or view the leaderboard, Google Play Games Services processes your Google account information and game scores according to Google's own Privacy Policy.

You can decline to sign in to Google Play Games; the game is fully playable without it, except the leaderboard feature will be unavailable.

Data stored on your device

Your current session's score card image (when you use "Challenge a friend") is written temporarily to app-private storage on your device so it can be shared through Android's share sheet. It is not uploaded anywhere by the app itself.

Children's privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same URL with a new "last updated" date.
