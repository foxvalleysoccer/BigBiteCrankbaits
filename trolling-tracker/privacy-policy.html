Privacy Policy for Trolling Tracker: Troll Speed

Last updated: July 1, 2026

Big Bite Crankbaits provides the Trolling Tracker: Troll Speed Android application. This policy explains how the app accesses, uses, shares, retains, and protects information.

Information used by the app

  • Precise location: used to calculate speed, display your position, record fishing routes, and save waypoints.
  • Photos: only photos you choose to attach to a waypoint or catch.
  • Fishing records: app settings, trips, tracks, waypoint notes, catch information, and GPS diagnostic logs.
  • Advertising information: Google Mobile Ads may process device identifiers, IP address, approximate location, app information, and ad interactions.

How information is used

Location and fishing information support the app's speedometer, maps, route recording, trip history, and waypoint features. GPS diagnostic logs help troubleshoot inaccurate or interrupted readings when that setting is enabled.

Local storage and sharing

Saved tracks, waypoint notes, catch details, diagnostic logs, and attached photos remain locally on your device. Big Bite Crankbaits does not sell these records or operate a server that receives them. Information is shared only when you deliberately use a device sharing feature or when advertising providers process ad-related data as described below.

Advertising and consent

The app uses Google AdMob. Google and its advertising partners may process advertising identifiers and the technical information described above to deliver, measure, secure, and limit advertising. Where required, Google's User Messaging Platform presents privacy choices before ads are requested. Available choices can be revisited from the app's Settings screen.

Learn more in Google's explanation of data used by partner apps and sites.

Retention and deletion

Fishing information remains on your device until you delete it in the app, clear the app's storage, or uninstall the app. The app does not create user accounts. Ad-related information is retained according to Google's policies.

Security

Reasonable measures are used to protect locally stored information. Android cloud backup is disabled for the app to reduce unintended copying of saved fishing locations. No storage or transmission method can be guaranteed completely secure.

Children

The app is intended for a general fishing audience and is not directed to children under 13.

Policy changes

This policy may be updated when the app or legal requirements change. The current version and update date will remain available at this URL.
